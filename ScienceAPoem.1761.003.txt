124 HOPKINSON, FRANCIS. Science: a Poem. 19 pages. 4to, modern marbled
boards, calf back. Philadelphia: William Dunlap, 1762
FIRST EDITION of this rare poem, foreseeing a glorious future for the College of Philadelphia. Dedicated, appropriately, to Franklin, Provost William Smith, Hugh Williamson, Philip Syng, and many other colonial notables. Library label inside cover.
